Book series

This book series was created and inspired by the Eragon and Wings of fire book series. The concept of the story is that there was an ancient race of dragons that had been hunted to extinction but 4 eggs were hidden away where no one would find them. 900 years later 6 children find the eggs and have alerted another race of dragons of their existence, with a fight for their lives ahead of them they will have to learn to work together to survive.

Main human characters in the story

  3. Rudey
  4. Riley

  6. Evelynn
